Continu User Dashboard

The new Continu Dashboard was designed for users to monitor their learning tracks, conversations and settings on the LMS. We set out to give the user a seamless experience within the app, track progress and control their settings.

Design Role

  • UX Design
  • Visual Design

Deliverables

  • Userflows
  • Wireframes
  • Visual Design
  • Prototype

Problem

Employees at companies like Airbnb and Eventbrite did not have a central location in the app to track all of their learning needs.

Solution

Employees can now view their Learning Tracks & activity throughout the Continu application. They can view upcoming events, track conversations and retrace liked content in the system.

User Stories and Flows

Now that we had an understanding that both the employer and employee have a need for custom Learning Tracks within the organization we start to create user stories and flows to highlight the main features of the application.

As an Employee I would like to access my settings in my user dashboard and add my slack profile.

Sketches

A vital part of my process is working through conceptual thinking in sketching. Quick and dirty ideation is always preferred over more complex, timely design techniques.

Lessons Learned

Learning Tracks fit a very specific need within an organization - to create and track engagement of custom courses. It is very important to have a seamless learning experience for employees if they are to interact with your content in an effective way. This product has become very important during the pandemic to help train employees for remote working.
