About Chris

I'm a UX Designer with a passion for how design and technology intertwine to solve problems for everyday users. I enjoy working in collaborative environments with design & development teams to execute and implement solutions. I have over 10 years experience designing for startups, as well as global corporations.

Over the past few years I have mentored many aspiring UX Designers at Bloc and Thinkful in the UX Designer Track program. These students are now solving complex problems at companies like Airbnb, IBM, Shopify, Facebook, Microsoft, Bank of America, Crate & Barrel and more.

I live on beautiful Cape Cod with my lovely wife Jessica and two rescue dogs Danny (saying Hi above) & Barnum.
