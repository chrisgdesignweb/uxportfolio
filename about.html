About Chris

Senior UX Designer with over 10 years of experience creating user-centered solutions for digital products. Demonstrated ability to lead cross-functional teams in delivering intuitive user experiences aligned with business goals. Expertise in UX research, UI design, design strategy, and design thinking, with a focus on fostering collaborative and innovative design cultures.

Design Philosophy

Focus on the Problem, Not Solutions

Clearly define the problem before proposing solutions.

Advocate for the User

Align your user needs with the business objectives.

Collaborate Early & Often

Engage the team and stakeholders from the start to ensure alignment and enthusiasm.

Research and Test

Engage users early to identify root causes and evaluate solutions.

Measure and Iterate

Set project goals, measure outcomes, and iterate based on data insights.

Leverage Design Insights

Thoroughly analyze user feedback to gain insights into diverse perspectives.
