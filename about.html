A little more about me

I am an end-to-end product designer with a proven track record in designing complex products, including Business Intelligence (BI) tools, Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems, and Learning Management Systems (LMS). Most recently, I collaborated with a startup on a patient-facing responsive web application, focusing on accessibility and inclusive design.

My design career began at a Swedish company, where I established a strong foundation in design principles rooted in Swiss Design. Our logos were designed by Paul Rand, Helvetica was our typeface, and we let content shine in everything we produced. My expertise spans the entire design process, encompassing branding, design direction, user research, visual interface design, prototyping, usability testing, and even some front-end coding with HTML and CSS.

I am passionate about creating accessible, user-friendly designs that effectively solve business challenges and drive company growth.
