Continu Learning Tracks

Learning Tracks gives course creators at organizations like Airbnb and Eventbrite the flexibility to create engaging course experiences. Course creators can also track course engagement & skill development.

Design Role

  • UX Design
  • Visual Design

Deliverables

  • Personas
  • Userflows
  • Wireframes
  • Pattern Library
  • Visual Design

Problem

Clients require the ability to create custom learning experiences for their employees that include a rich user experience where engagement is tracked.

Solution

Administrators create Learning Tracks seamlessly through the Continu application and monitor engagement. Employees take courses called Learning Tracks on topics pertaining to their job and organization.

Lessons Learned

Learning Tracks fit a very specific need within an organization - to create and track engagement of custom courses. It is very important to have a seamless learning experience for employees if they are to interact with your content in an effective way. Organizations continue to use this product during the pandemic to help train employees for remote working.

We learned as product team that the inconsistency of design elements within the larger application was detracting from the user experience. The use of a pattern library helped solve some of these recurring issues. It took time and effort to create the pattern library, but it resolved the issues we were having.
