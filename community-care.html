Community Care Platform for Patients with Social Determinants of Health (SDOH)

Unfortunately, I am unable to showcase the work I completed during my 2.5 years at Activate Care due to privacy restrictions. During this time, I served as the Senior UX Designer on the Solutions Team, which was comprised of UX Design, UX Research, Product, and our Care Team. In this role, I spearheaded the design of numerous projects and made significant accomplishments. Below, I will provide a concise overview of some of the key projects I worked on during this period.

Social Needs Screening Tool

  • Collaborated with the Product and Engineering teams to release a new patient-facing screening tool to enhance the efficiency and workload capacity of our Community Health Workers.
  • Standardized the design using our new design system to enable quick builds for future surveys and assessments, ensuring accessibility and scalability as the foundation of the Patient Web Application.
  • Worked with the Lead UX Researcher to conduct usability testing on our initial solution, which informed improvements for the next version.
  • Presented the Product team with an early MVP that could be quickly developed by engineering, while simultaneously delivering a second version to Product incorporating ideal UI/UX patterns for surveys.
  • Planned component work to initiate our new Design System, marking the start of this significant project.
  • To streamline design and development handoff and collaboration, I introduced the company to Zeplin, improving efficiency in our processes.

Digital Design Brand Refresh and Design System for Accessibility 

  • Collaborated with the Lead UX Researcher to evaluate our current offerings for accessibility, which is critical for our company as we operate in the social care market.
  • Developed new branding assets with a focus on accessibility, including a new design system, updated color palettes, and typography
  • Successfully presented this work to our senior leadership and C-suite, gaining approval to revamp our digital assets with an emphasis on accessibility to facilitate the scalability of our products. Additionally, I worked with the engineering team to gain support for a new component library using MUI and Joy UI, a project that is currently in progress.
  • These efforts have not only opened the door to greater accessibility across our products but have also trimmed design maintenance costs, with the most noticeable gains seen in the newly launched Screening Tool and Patient Web Application.

Patient Communication, SMS Messaging

  • Introduced UX workshops to the company to involve more employees in the UX process and enhance the organization's UX maturity. For this project, I conducted Empathy Mapping, Proto Persona, and Scenario Mapping workshops to help everyone across the product team better understand our patients. Participants included members from the Care Team, Product, Engineering, Operations, and Marketing.
  • Researched best practices for SMS campaigns in healthcare and designed a program to achieve our initial SMS target engagement rate
  • Successfully designed an internal feature for our Community Health Workers to send SMS messages and created a new “Help” panel within the internal platform to assist external users in quickly onboarding to using SMS with their patients.
  • We achieved our target engagement rate in the first quarter of launching our pilot program with patients.

Patient Web Application 

  • Collaborated closely with the Product and Engineering teams to design a comprehensive MVP for our new application. This involved aligning on strategic goals, defining user requirements, and iterating on design concepts to ensure the product met both user needs and business objectives.
  • In partnership with the Lead UX Researcher, I crafted an engaging onboarding experience to address and reduce user drop-off, which had been a significant issue in previous applications. We conducted user research and testing to identify friction points in the onboarding process, implementing design solutions that enhance user understanding and satisfaction.
  • Designed an in-app messaging system to facilitate communication beyond SMS, building on a previous project I led. This system was developed to provide users with a seamless communication experience, integrating new features that support real-time interactions and improve overall user engagement.
  • Throughout these projects, I ensured that all design decisions aligned with the company's broader goals, leveraging user insights to create a cohesive and user-friendly experience across our digital platforms.
